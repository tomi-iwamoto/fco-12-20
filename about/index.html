about

Marina Maria is a radio host of an international Christian radio program, Faith City Outreach, which helps market Christian pastors and leaders to advertise their ministries.

The global internet radio program is aired on Evangelism Radio, which airs to over 160 countries and 50 states. It is the leading Christian internet and global radio station in the world.

As of 2020, Marina is the Ambassador USW-CCC Native American National Director.

Also, a bilingual published poet of two poetry books by a publishing company called, The Book's Mind. In 2015, Seasons, and 2016, Fruit of the Spirit books were published.

In addition, Marina is involved in a missions prayer team ministry co-sponsored by Four Winds Ministries, where a group of pastors and Christian leaders travel to Native American reservations in Arizona to pray for pastors at their church.

Marina and her husband, Louie, are cyclists, live in Glendale, Arizona, are happily married, and have two children.
